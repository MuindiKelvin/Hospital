Mwananchi Natinal Hospital started in 2012 under the management of U.S.A NGO(FreeHealthcareOrganization) with the the aim of providing affordable health care program to each citizen in kenya.Basically,through the free health care program many kenyans have been helped mostly mothers,children and the disabled personnel. The free health care program runs across the country and this came up due to the rise of hospitality bills which through the research before the program was initiated, we found that hospitals basically were not accessed by every citizen though they are govt sponsored. Many of our citizens have beneficiated from the free health program since we offer free cancer scans for everybody,free maternal support. We still do support the disabled by providing everything they need. Since some areas across the country lack proper infrastructure,we always rise some amounts from well wishers inorder to support our work of helping the needy in those areas.
